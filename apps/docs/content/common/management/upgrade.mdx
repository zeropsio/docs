---
title: How to upgrade a service version
description: Learn how to upgrade your service's version
---

You can upgrade or downgrade your service to a different major version by setting the <a href="/zerops-yml/specification#base-optional">`run.base`</a> parameter in your `zerops.yml`. When you [trigger a new pipeline](/nodejs/how-to/trigger-pipeline), Zerops will start new runtime container(s) with the required version. If you don't specify the `run.base` attribute in your `zerops.yml`, Zerops keeps the current version of your runtime.

If you want to build your application with a different major Node.js version, change the <a href="/zerops-yml/specification#base-required">`build.base`</a> parameter in your `zerops.yml`. The `build.base` is the required attribute.
